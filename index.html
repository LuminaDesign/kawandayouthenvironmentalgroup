Promote Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Through Education and Training:

We focuses on creating awareness and providing education on sustainable practices. By offering training programs and educational resources, the organization aims to empower individuals and communities to adopt and implement sustainable practices. This includes understanding and applying concepts related to climate resilience, mental well-being, and social equity

Enhance Climate Resilience in Disadvantaged Communities:

We strive to help communities adapt to the impacts of climate change. This involves implementing projects that improve environmental practices, such as sustainable agriculture, waste management, and reforestation efforts. By focusing on adaptation strategies, the organization aims to reduce vulnerabilities and build resilience in communities that are most affected by climate change.

Support Youth Development Through Skills, Education, and Resources:

Explanation: This objective is centered around empowering young people by providing them with the necessary skills, education, and resources to reach their full potential. This includes offering training programs, educational scholarships, and resources that enhance employability and entrepreneurial skills. The aim is to equip youth with the tools they need to contribute effectively to their communities and drive positive change.

Our Projects

We work on a variety of projects that help disadvantaged communities adapt to climate change, improve the mental well-being of young people, and provide youth with the skills, education, and resources they need to reach their full potential.

Community Tree Planting

Our tree planting projects aim to restore deforested areas, improve air quality, and combat climate change.

Environmental Education

We provide educational programs to raise awareness about environmental protection and sustainable practices.

Waste Management

Our waste management initiatives focus on reducing, reusing, and recycling waste to create cleaner communities.

Climate Resilience Training

We provide training on how to adapt to the impacts of climate change, focusing on sustainable agricultural practices.

About Us

Kawanda Youth Environmental Group is focused on prevention-focused mental well-being projects, adaptation-focused climate resilience projects, and projects enabling social equity through education, employability, and entrepreneurship solutions.

Mission:

To build a community dedicated to improving the mental well-being of young people and helping disadvantaged communities adapt to climate change by providing youth with the skills, education, and resources they need to reach their full potential.
